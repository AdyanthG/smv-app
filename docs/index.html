Common questions

How are scores calculated? On-device computer vision analyzes facial geometry and proportions. Scores are algorithmic estimates for entertainment and self-improvement — not a judgment of your worth.

How do I delete my account? Settings → Account Actions → Delete Account. This permanently removes your data.

How do I manage my subscription? Settings app → your Apple ID → Subscriptions.

How do I report content? Tap the ••• menu on any post → Report, or Block the user.
