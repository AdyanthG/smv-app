Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Face Scan Data: When you scan, your face is analyzed on-device using Apple's Vision framework. Raw facial-landmark geometry is processed locally and is not sent to our servers.
  • Scan Scores: Your computed scores and attribute breakdowns are stored in our cloud database (Firebase Firestore), linked to your account ID.
  • Scan Images: If you opt in, scan images are uploaded to secure cloud storage (Firebase Storage) for your history and social features. Images are encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • Account Information: Display name, handle, and authentication tokens via Sign in with Apple or anonymous sign-in. Email if you use Sign in with Apple.
  • User Content: Posts, comments, and other content you create.
  • Usage Data: App interactions and analytics used to improve the experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate and display your scores and breakdowns
  • To power leaderboards, voting, and the social feed
  • To enable community features (posts, comments, follows)
  • To deliver notifications you opt into
  • To process referrals and premium subscriptions
  • To improve scoring accuracy and app performance

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure.
  • Face analysis is performed on-device — we never send raw facial-landmark data to external servers.
  • All network traffic uses TLS encryption.
  • Firebase Security Rules restrict data access to authenticated users.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with service providers strictly to operate the app: Google Firebase (cloud storage, authentication, messaging) and Apple (sign-in and in-app purchases). We may disclose information if required by law.

5. Your Rights

  • Delete Account: Settings → Account Actions → Delete Account permanently removes your data, including a server-side cleanup of your scans, posts, and images.
  • Export Data: Contact us to request a data export.
  • Opt Out: You can use the app without creating posts or making your profile public.

6. Age Requirement

SMV is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 17.

7. Contact

For privacy inquiries: support@smvapp.com
